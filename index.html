Questions, Answered

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Really free, no catch. Jake builds valuations at no cost because some homeowners who get one eventually decide to sell — and when they do, he'd love the chance to earn that business. If you never sell, you still keep the report. That's the whole model.

Am I obligated to sell or list with Jake?

No. Zero obligation. Most people who request a valuation aren't selling any time soon — they're just curious, refinancing, or planning ahead. That's exactly what this is for.

Will I get spammed or blasted with calls?

No. You'll get your valuation report by email within 24 hours. After that, Jake sends one short market update per month — real numbers on what Colorado Springs homes are actually selling for, so your valuation never goes stale. Don't want it? One click unsubscribes. If you gave a phone number, Jake may follow up once to make sure you got your report. No daily calls, no drip campaigns.

How is this more accurate than Zillow?

Zestimates are built from public records by an algorithm that has never seen your home. Jake pulls actual recent closed sales near you, adjusts for your home's condition, upgrades, and location factors, and shows you the comps so you can check the math yourself.

What if I'm not selling for a year or more?

Even better — knowing your number early means you can plan improvements, timing, and finances with real data instead of guessing. Request it now, use it whenever you're ready.
